Discover Aba, Nigeria

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Discover Aba, Nigeria

I chose this subject because Aba is my hometown and is known as one of Nigeria's most important commercial centers. Creating this website will allow me to share information about the city's culture, economy, and attractions while practicing my web development skills.

Site Purpose

This website will provide information about Aba, a major commercial city in Nigeria.

  • An overview of the city's history and location
  • Popular markets and business activities
  • Tourist attractions and landmarks
  • Local culture, food, and traditions
  • Photo gallery of important places in Aba
  • Contact and feedback form for visitors

Scenarios

  • What are the most popular markets and business centers in Aba?
  • What tourist attractions and landmarks can I visit in Aba?
  • What traditional foods and cultural activities are unique to Aba?
  • How can I contact the website owner to provide feedback?

Color Schema

  • Dark Green (#006400) - Headings, navigation, and footer.
  • Gold (#FFD700) - Highlights, buttons, and accents.
  • White (#FFFFFF) - Background and content areas.

Typography

  • Montserrat - Headings and navigation.
  • Roboto - Body text and paragraphs.
